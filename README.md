# ChurnIQ — Churn Risk Dashboard

A subscription churn risk analyzer for product managers and customer success teams. Upload customer data or use sample data to identify at-risk accounts and get actionable retention recommendations.

## Live Demo
[churn-risk-dashboard.vercel.app](https://churn-risk-dashboard.vercel.app)

## Features
- Automated churn risk scoring (Health Score 0-100)
- Risk breakdown by High / Medium / Low segments
- Revenue at risk analysis
- Customer detail modal with risk factors and recommended actions
- CSV upload support (drag & drop or browse)
- Filter by risk level and plan type
- Export filtered data to CSV
- Mark customers as contacted or dismiss risk
- Persistent state via localStorage

## Tech Stack
React, Recharts, PapaParse, Vite, Vercel
